FAQ

  • How do I get extra lives?

    Lives replenish over time or can be requested from friends.

  • Can I play offline?

    The game can be played offline, but some features require an internet connection.

  • What are the best strategies for higher scores?

    Combining multiple power-ups and planning moves ahead can maximize your score.

  • Are there any in-app purchases?

    Yes, the game offers in-app purchases but they are not necessary to progress.

  • How do I unlock new levels?

    Complete existing levels to progress or unlock with in-app purchases.

  • Can I play with friends?

    Yes, connect to social media to compete with friends.

  • What happens if I switch devices?

    Sync your game via social media to save progress across devices.

  • Is there customer support available?

    Yes, support can be contacted through the game's settings for any issues.

  • How often are new levels added?

    New levels and content are added regularly.

  • Are there special events?

    Yes, special events and challenges are available during holidays and more.
