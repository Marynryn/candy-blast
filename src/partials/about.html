About

"Beagle Universe" offers a delightful match-3 experience where players help a crew of adventurous beagles travel through magical lands.

With over 500 levels, each filled with unique puzzles and colorful obstacles, players will enjoy an engaging narrative that unfolds with each victory.

The game combines traditional match-3 mechanics with innovative power-ups and a charming storyline about friendship and adventure.

The visual design features vibrant graphics and adorable characters, making it a family-friendly title that appeals to both young players and adult puzzle enthusiasts alike.
